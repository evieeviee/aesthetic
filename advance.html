Frequently Asked Questions

Before your consultation

Clear information helps clients feel more prepared and comfortable before visiting the clinic.

Do I need a consultation before treatment?

Consultation is recommended, particularly for first-time clients or when considering a new treatment.

How do I know which treatment is suitable?

A practitioner should assess your concerns, goals and relevant history before recommending an option.

How long does an appointment take?

Appointment time varies by treatment. Most sample treatments shown here take between 45 and 90 minutes.

Can results vary?

Yes. Results differ depending on individual skin condition, treatment choice, response and aftercare.

Can I reschedule my appointment?

Yes. Contact the clinic as early as possible so another appointment time can be arranged.

What should I prepare before consultation?

Bring a list of current products, relevant treatment history and the concerns you would like to discuss.

Can I ask questions through WhatsApp first?

Yes. WhatsApp can be used for general enquiries and appointment coordination. Detailed assessment should be completed during consultation.
