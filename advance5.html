Questions & Clarity

Frequently Asked.

Are consultations conducted by registered doctors? +
Yes, every single consultation and medical procedure at Noir Aesthetics is performed directly by our MOH-registered, LCP-certified aesthetic physicians.
Is there downtime for Pico Laser treatments? +
Standard Pico laser toning has zero to minimal downtime (mild redness for 2-4 hours). Fractional treatments for deep acne scars may cause light pin-point flaking for 3-5 days.
How long do Ultherapy® results last? +
Ultherapy® stimulates natural neocollagenesis. Most patients enjoy visible lifting and tightening results for 12 to 18 months following a single protocol.
What payment methods and installment plans are available? +
We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and offer 0% interest installment plans for up to 12 or 24 months through major Malaysian banking partners.
