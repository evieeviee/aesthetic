Pico Laser Treatment in Kuala Lumpur

Pico Laser is the gold-standard laser technology in Malaysia for treating obstinate pigmentation, acne scars, and enlarged pores. Utilizing picosecond laser pulses, it shatters melanin particles without causing heat damage to surrounding skin tissue.

Key Benefits

  • ✓ Targets stubborn Asian Melasma & Post-Inflammatory Hyperpigmentation (PIH)
  • ✓ Stimulates elastin and collagen for pore refinement
  • ✓ Minimal to zero downtime (mild pinkish flush for 2–4 hours)
  • ✓ Administered strictly by MOH-licensed medical doctors

Frequently Asked Questions (Pico Laser KL)

How many Pico Laser sessions do I need for melasma?

Most patients in Kuala Lumpur see visible brightening after 1 to 2 sessions. However, a clinical protocol of 4 to 6 sessions spaced 3 weeks apart is recommended for severe pigmentation.
